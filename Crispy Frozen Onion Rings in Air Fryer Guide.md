## How to Cook Frozen Onion Rings in an Air Fryer

Cooking **frozen onion rings in an air fryer** is straightforward, but a few key steps ensure perfect results. Follow this step-by-step guide for crispy, delicious onion rings every time.

### Ingredients and Tools
- Frozen onion rings (your preferred brand)
- Non-stick cooking spray or a light drizzle of olive oil (optional, for extra crispiness)
- Air fryer (any model works, but basket-style air fryers are most common)
- Tongs for flipping
- Serving plate or bowl

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. **Preheat Your Air Fryer**: Set your air fryer to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C) and let it preheat for 3-5 minutes. Preheating ensures even cooking and maximum crispiness.
2. **Arrange the Onion Rings**: Place the frozen onion rings in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Avoid overcrowding to allow proper air circulation. If needed, cook in batches.
3. **Add a Light Spray of Oil (Optional)**: For extra crunch, lightly spray the onion rings with non-stick cooking spray or drizzle with a teaspoon of olive oil.
4. **Cook the Onion Rings**: Air fry for 5-7 minutes, shaking the basket or flipping the rings halfway through to ensure even cooking. Check for your desired level of crispiness.
5. **Check and Season**: Once done, transfer the onion rings to a serving plate. Taste and sprinkle with a pinch of salt or your favorite seasoning, if desired.
6. **Serve Immediately**: Enjoy your **air fryer frozen onion rings** hot with your favorite dipping sauce, like ketchup, ranch, or spicy mayo.

### Cooking Times by Air Fryer Type
- **Basket Air Fryer**: 5-7 minutes at 400¬∞F, shaking halfway.
- **Oven-Style Air Fryer**: 7-10 minutes at 400¬∞F, flipping halfway.
- **Small Air Fryers**: May require multiple batches to avoid overcrowding.
